Human & Nature

by Yeqian Li

This is a childrenâ€™s picture book. Our bodies have many similarities with nature. For example, the texture of our skin looks like bark, and our spines have many similarities with fern. Not only that, we come from nature and will return to nature after death, which I think is 'human nature'. I hope to show that we are one with nature by connecting the human body with nature. This project is about exploring the commonalities between the human body and nature. I hope this picture book will provide new perspective for children to see the world, having their own understanding of nature and the their body.
