The Puppet Museum

by Yeqian Li

My initial inspiration for this project was from a puppet toy of mine.The fact that puppets can be manipulated made me think about the compromises and changes I made to adapt to life as I grew up in the face of the environment and people around me, and I lost my true self in the process.I used picture books to show the five stages of my growth from a child to a puppet.
